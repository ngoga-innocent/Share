Water:

Wherever there is a shortage of clean water, we are committed to building and maintaining facilities that allow everyone to access it, thus meeting a basic human need, while ensuring improved hygiene and sanitation conditions for people.

Food:

IThe lack of adequate nutrition is a threat to the normal development of physical, intellectual and cultural capacities of many people, especially children. This is why we are committed to building community canteens and promoting organic farming projects.

Education:

Going to school is an alternative to child labor and exploitation, a hope to escape the grip of ignorance and poverty, and the possibility of creating the foundations for a better future. This is why we are committed to building schools and providing educational support for hundreds of students

Work:

In order to create opportunities for decent work and improve the living standards of the poorest families, we organize vocational training courses, promote crafts and small cooperative enterprises, including through the granting of non-profit credits.

Health:

thanks to the collaboration of doctors, therapists and operators, we carry out projects in defense of the common good of health: medical assistance interventions, meetings, seminars and training courses capable of providing simple but effective tools, capable of enhancing the resources of local culture.

Culture:

we believe that in the process of evolution of society it is of fundamental importance to respect and preserve the cultural biodiversity of the communities that populate our planet, as if they were so many treasures of humanity. For this reason we are committed to their protection and their free development through the creation of Centers for the Culture of the Common Good.
